What is Fox Night?

Fox Night is a weekly virtual meet hosted by @Edwin the Fox in which participants either watch a movie, or play a fun game together. Every week, we rotate activities - this website is used to watch a movie! The event starts at 9:30 AM PST and usually lasts for an hour or until the movie ends
